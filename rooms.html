ROOMS & SUITES

SKT. PETRI offers unique and spacious rooms and suites furnished with an eye for laid-back luxury, but don’t worry, we’ve got plenty of rooms to choose from – all designed in a Scandinavian style with an international touch. Oh, and not to forget: The spectacular view looking over Copenhagen. Welcome to SKT. PETRI.

STANDARD ROOM

With 20-24 sqm (215-260 sqf) our standard room isn’t the biggest room we have to offer, but still has all the feature you need. The room is perfect for one or two guests.
